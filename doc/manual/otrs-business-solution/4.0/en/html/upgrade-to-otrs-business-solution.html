Chapter 2. Upgrading to OTRS Business Solution™

In order to upgrade to OTRS Business Solution™, you need a valid contract with the OTRS group. If you already have a contract, you can upgrade to OTRS Business Solution™ by visiting the AdminOTRSBusiness screen in your OTRS instance. If you're entitled to upgrade, you will (as an admin user) also see a notification bar which guides you to the upgrade screen. The upgrade itself is done automatically by just pressing the upgrade button.

If you have any questions regarding your entitlement for the OTRS Business Solution™ or technical questions, please contact sales@otrs.com.
