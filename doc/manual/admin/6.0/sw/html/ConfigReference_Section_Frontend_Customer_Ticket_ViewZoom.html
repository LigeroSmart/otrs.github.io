Frontend → Customer → Ticket → ViewZoom

Ticket::Frontend::CustomerTicketZoom###ArticleType

Fafanua aina ya chaguo-msingi ya kidokezo katika skrini ya tiketi iliyokuzwa ya kiolesura cha mteja.

This setting can not be deactivated.

Thamani chaguo-msingi: 

--- webrequest

Ticket::Frontend::CustomerTicketZoom###AttributesView

Inaonyesha sifa za tiketi zilizoamishwa katika kiolesura cha mteja (0 = kutowezesha na 1 = Wezesha).

This setting can not be deactivated.

Thamani chaguo-msingi: 

---
Owner: '0'
Priority: '1'
Queue: '1'
Responsible: '0'
SLA: '0'
Service: '0'
State: '1'
Type: '0'
